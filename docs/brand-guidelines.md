# Brand Guidelines\n\nGuideliness for maintaining Elevate IR's visual identity across all digital properties.\n\n## Brand Identity\n\n**Elevate IR** is a strategic financial communications and investor relations firm. Our design reflects:\n\n- Professional trust and credibility\n- Modern sophistication\n- Clear communication\n- Refined elegance\n\n## Color Palette\n\n### Primary Colors\n\n| Color | Hex | Use |\n|-------|-----|-----|\n| Black | #000000 | Headers, main backgrounds, primary text |\n| Elevate Green | #58B51A | CTAs, interactive elements, highlights |\n\n### Neutral Colors\n\n| Color | Hex | Use |\n|-------|-----|-----|\n| Cream | #EDE9D7 | Secondary backgrounds, accents |\n| Gray | #9D9D9D | Secondary text, disabled states |\n| White | #FFFFFF | Primary background |\n\n### Semantic Colors\n\n| State | Hex | Use |\n|-------|-----|-----|\n| Success | #58B51A | Positive confirmations, success messages |\n| Warning | #F59E0B | Alerts, cautions, pending actions |\n| Error | #EF4444 | Errors, destructive actions |\n| Info | #3B82F6 | Informational messages |\n\n## Typography\n\n### Headings\n\n- **Font:** Raleway\n- **Weight:** SemiBold (600) or Bold (700)\n- **Style:** Modern, clean, professional\n- **Usage:** Page titles, section headers, prominent calls-to-action\n\n### Body Text\n\n- **Font:** Open Sans\n- **Weight:** Regular (400) or Medium (500)\n- **Line Height:** 1.5 (normal) for body, 1.2 (tight) for headings\n- **Usage:** Paragraphs, descriptions, all readable content\n\n## Visual Style\n\n### Spacing\n\nFollows an 8px grid system:\n- Extra Small: 4px\n- Small: 8px\n- Medium: 16px\n- Large: 24px\n- Extra Large: 32px+\n\n### Border Radius\n\n- Small elements: 4px\n- Medium elements: 8px\n- Large elements: 12px\n- Pills/full circles: 9999px\n\n### Shadows\n\n- Subtle (sm): `0 1px 2px 0 rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)`\n- Medium (md): `0 4px 6px -1px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1)`\n- Strong (lg): `0 10px 15px -3px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1)`\n\n## Do's and Don'ts\n\n### Do's ✅\n\n- Use plenty of whitespace\n- Maintain consistent spacing\n- Keep interfaces clean and uncluttered\n- Use brand colors purposefully\n- Ensure high contrast for readability\n- Test on multiple devices\n\n### Don'ts ❌\n\n- Don't use off-brand colors\n- Don't crowd content\n- Don't use multiple competing typefaces\n- Don't sacrifice accessibility for aesthetics\n- Don't use low contrast combinations\n- Don't add unnecessary animations\n
